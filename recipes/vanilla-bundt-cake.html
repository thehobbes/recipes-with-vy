Vanilla Bundt Cake

Description

Made with Vy in preparation for Christmas.

Ingredients

Cake

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon basking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, room temp
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 6 large eggs, room temp
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup whole milk

Glaze

  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 350 deg F.
  2. Whisk flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat butter and cream cheese until smooth. Add sugar and beat until light and fluffy
  4. Incorporate 3 eggs one at a time. Add 3 tablespoons of the drys. Incorporate other 3 eggs. Beat in vanilla.
  5. Add 1/3 drys, mix until combined. Add half of the milk, mix until combined. Repeat, alternating drys and milk.
  6. Butter and flour bundt pan.
  7. Pour in cake batter and gently tap on counter to settle.
  8. Bake for 45 minutes until toothpick comes out clean.
  9. Cool for 20 minutes on cooling rack.

Glaze

  1. Whisk powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla until smooth.
  2. Pour over cooled cake.
