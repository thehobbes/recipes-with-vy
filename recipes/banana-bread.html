Banana Bread

Description

For bananas that have grown ripe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teapspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 large beaten eggs
  • 2+1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly 9x5-inch grease pan.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Beat brown sugar and butter with an electric mixer in a separate large bowl until smooth. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended.
  4. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture until just combined. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 60 minutes.
  6. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
