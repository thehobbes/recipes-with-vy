Chocolate-Chipless Cookies

Description

For those whose favorite part of the cookie is the dough...

Or if you don't have any chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg + 1 egg yolk, room temp
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 7/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda [x3 for powder]
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Brown the butter over medium heat until nutty aroma - take off heat immediately.
  2. Combine brown butter, brown sugar, and white sugar. Beat until mixed.
  3. Add in the egg, egg yolk, and vanilla. Mix well.
  4. In another bowl, mix flour, salt, and baking soda.
  5. Mix half the drys into the wets. Slowly add the drys until the dough gets dry.
  6. Refrigerate for 30+ minutes.
  7. Preheat oven to 350 deg F. Bake for 11 minutes. Let cool before eating.
