Butterscotch Cinnamon Pie

Description

A dessert from Undertale and my favorite pie. Baked for Thanksgiving.

Ingredients

Filling and Crust

  • 1 premade crust
  • 5/4 cups whole milk
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • 3 egg yolks, lightly beaten
  • 1/3 cup cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Topping [Optional]

  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • ground cinnamon for sprinkling

Directions

Crust

  1. Fill in pie with weights like beans to prevent warping.
  2. Bake crust at 375 deg F for 20-25 minutes.
  3. Remove weights and bake for 5-10 minutes until golden brown.
  4. Remove from oven, set aside.

Filling

  1. In a pan over medium heat, whisk milk, cream, brown sugar, cornstarch, salt, & cinnamon. Stir until pudding-like
  2. Slowly temper egg yolks, by adding spoonfuls of the mixture to the yolks. Keep whisking.
  3. Gradually add yolk mixture back to the pan and continue whisking until very thick.
  4. Remove from heat, stir in butter and vanilla until fully encorporated.

Assembly

  1. Pour filling into crust.
  2. Let cool for 30 minutes, then refrigerate for 6+ hours
  3. Stephen: bake in oven for 45 minutes.
  4. [Optional] Whip whipping cream and sugar. Spread on pie. Sprinkle ground cinnamon.
